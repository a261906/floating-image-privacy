Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Floating Image is developed by Xinyi Tools.

Information We Collect

Floating Image does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Images Selected by Users

Floating Image lets users select images from their device and display them as floating overlays. Selected images are stored locally on the user's device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Permissions

Floating Image may request the following permissions or system access:

  • Appear on top: Used to display floating images and controls over other apps.
  • Notifications: Used only to show optional app controls in the notification area when enabled by the user.
  • Foreground service: Used to keep the floating image control available while the user is using other apps, when enabled by the user.
  • Photos or media access: Used only when the user selects an image to display as a floating overlay.

Purchases

Floating Image uses Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases. Payment information is handled by Google Play and is not collected by us.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

Children

Floating Image is not directed to children under 13.
